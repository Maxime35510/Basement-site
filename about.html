Who are we?

Basement Developers is a Discord server, its purpose is to connect developers around the world. We help newcomers and advanced users with their codes, generally helping them in their way to improve their Discord Bot. Our community is a safe and healthy place, thanks to our Moderation team, and the awesome members of the community.
