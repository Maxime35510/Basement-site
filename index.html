About us

We are Basement Network, a group that creates and help communities in social medias. Why we do it? well, we want to give an opportunity to whoever wants to create their own original community. If you ever tried to create a community, you must know how hard it can be; Getting the staff, the server design, the promotion to grow the community... We want to simplify these things for everyone who wants to make original communities.

Design

We have a long template list for any kind of community you want! just pick the one you most like and start building.

Staff

We have a lot of voluntary trusted staffs that may be interested on being part of your awesome community!

Promotion

We will take care of the promotion, you will be on our site as a Partner, and in our others servers!

Website

You don't need to worry about web development! we will host a website for you in one of our amazing sub-domains (server.basement-network.com)

Custom bots

If your community gets bigger, you will need more than other people's bots, what about a custom one?

Programs

Periodically, we do special programs and events, you can participate in these programs to grow side-by-side with our others partners.

What's the trick?

Now you must be asking yourself, what's the trick? do I need to sell my soul? do I need to pay something? And then we suddenly answer, "no!" everything is free, and you must be thinking *Impossible!* Well, ain't lying, we won't ask you for a single penny, at least not a non-optional one-. Jokes aside, we pay all we need to maintain the website with the ad revenue of our sites, and optional users donations. So yes, it's free for you, there's no trick in our purposes, we want to make communities around the world; For the users, for you, for everyone.
